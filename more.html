In addition to promoting a cleansed environment and an overall better world, we at CleanEarth support a reinvigorated spirit. Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It is evident that mental health plays a crucial role in our everyday lives, and imperative that we learn to take care of it.

Break the Stigma: Brought to you by Children's Hospital Colorado

We as a people subconsciously think of mental health in a stigmatic manner. Like, if you are depressed then you are always sad, or constantly taking medication. Some might even group a mental illness with an insane asylum, and even the phrase "mental illness" itself tends to be used in a derogatory manner. Social media widely impacts our generation in a number of ways, and now more than ever. It creates the false perception that everyone's life is great, forcing us to think "so maybe there's just something wrong with me". And no one would ever tell someone with a broken leg to just “get over it,” yet this is what people who suffer from mental health issues are told. Often times people tend to steer away from talking about mental health, but this is exactly what we should be doing. Talk.
