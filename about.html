About Project: CleanEarth


Mission Statement:


We believe that the CleanEarth program can revolutionize sanitation globally, through providing supplies and utilities that can be used to maintain a pristine environment. Our one for one program also provides a humanitarian aspect to our vision, by giving a box of supplies to people of struggling countries for every one that is purchased. Our Clean - Mind, Body & Soul program also supports mental health, a criminally undervalued aspect of one's bodily health.
