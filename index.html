What We Do

At Project: CleanEarth, we make all-natural sanitation products of many shapes and form, ranging from pet sanitation to laundry care. Our products are made with all natural ingredients and contain no Genetically Engineered compounds. The products also come in 100% biodegradable containers. We also have a subsciption programme availible called CleanEarth Plus.



CleanEarth Plus

As you know, we have a monthly subsciption programme called CleanEarth Plus. If you subscribe to this program,you recieve a monthly shipment of the newest sanitation products we make of each category. If we release a new product, a CleanEarth Plus subscriber will recieve a complimentary unit. At the register, you get 15% off all CleanEarth products, and additionally, you can choose to pay back the 15%, and that money will go towards supporting Sanitation efforts in high-risk communities in the U.S.
